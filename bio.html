Biography of Stephen Bonish

I grew up in Wilmington, MA with my family of 5. In my house was my two older brothers and my mother and father. As you can tell by my photo, I am quite strong. My entire life, I have done nothing but lift weights. I first went to the gym when I was 3 years old and I haven't missed a day since. Now, I compete at the World's Strongest Man competition every year. I have won this competition the last 3 years, and I believe I a am in shape to win again this coming year. I will update everyone after this year's competition, which is on November 16th. And that pretty much brings you up to the present with my life.
